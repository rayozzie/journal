I'm happy to say there really is no end to family stories, but how can I fittingly bring to a close the precious remembrances of our little family.

The love, care and concern Margaret, Frank and I received from our hardworking parents are unforgettable and as seen through my eyes, reminiscing about tender memories has taken me on a very sentimental and sometimes tearful journey.

While carefully studying photos and writing, our dear departed family members would briefly be here again for me and my emotions reacted.  My apologies for rambling on in a rather disorganized fashion.

Please know that dear Grandma and Grandpa began our very special family with their own unique life experiences and I encourage you to add your own sweet memories to this family story.

My love to you all.
